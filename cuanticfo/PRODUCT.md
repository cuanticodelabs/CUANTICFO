# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Business owners (dueños) of Chilean small and medium businesses who review their company's financial health. They are not accounting specialists but are responsible for understanding whether the business is profitable and solvent. They open CuantiCFO periodically — monthly close, tax prep, cash position checks — and need the answer fast. Their primary question every session: "Am I profitable this month, and do I have enough cash?"

## Product Purpose

CuantiCFO is a Chilean CFO platform for SMBs that consolidates financial management: ingresos, gastos, libros contables, cierre mensual, and F29 tax preparation. It replaces spreadsheets and disconnected SII filings with a single, coherent financial picture. Success means a business owner can open the dashboard and know their financial health in under 30 seconds, without needing their accountant on the phone.

## Brand Personality

Precise and expert. The design communicates competence through restraint. Not decorative, not playful — each element exists because it earns its place. Trust comes from accuracy and structural clarity, not visual warmth. Think Bloomberg Terminal or a well-engineered instrument: legibility is the identity. Three words: precise, clear, trustworthy.

## Anti-references

- **Generic B2B SaaS**: Salesforce, HubSpot, QuickBooks — navy blue, white card grids, icon badges, Tailwind-blue accent. The tool that looks like every other tool.
- **Consumer fintech**: Mint, Robinhood — colorful gradients, gamified streaks, pastel cards, friendly illustrations. Too casual for professional financial responsibility.
- **AI-generated dashboards**: The SaaS-cream hero-metric template — five identical KPI cards with big numbers, small labels, trend arrows, sparklines in rows of five. The default output when a model builds a dashboard without thinking.

## Design Principles

1. **Numbers are the product**: The interface exists to surface financial data. Decoration that doesn't aid comprehension is removed.
2. **Hierarchy through scale and structure**: Important metrics earn their size. Five equal-weight cards signal that everything is equally important, which means nothing is.
3. **Expert confidence**: The interface assumes the user is intelligent. Don't explain the obvious. Trust them to read a clear layout.
4. **Restraint as identity**: Distinctive through what we refuse to add — no gradient accents, no icon-card grids, no decorative borders. Clarity is the brand.
5. **Answer first**: The primary question ("Am I profitable? Do I have cash?") should be answered the moment the dashboard loads, without scanning.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Spanish (Chilean Spanish, es-CL). Respect prefers-reduced-motion. Income and expense encoding uses both color and directional icons, never color alone.
